Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship

Titan Glass & Metal was founded on a simple principle: every customer deserves precision work, honest pricing, and a contractor they can actually trust. Based in New Jersey, we specialize in custom glass and metal fabrication for both residential and commercial clients throughout the state.

From custom frameless shower enclosures and beveled mirrors to full commercial storefront glass installations, every project we take on is treated with the same unwavering attention to detail. We measure on-site, fabricate to exact specs, and install with care — because a perfect fit isn't optional, it's the standard.

When you work with Titan Glass & Metal, you're not getting a generic contractor. You're getting a craftsman who stands behind every cut, every seal, and every installation. That's the Titan difference.
